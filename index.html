Works 作品

Breaking

2026

Materials: Silver, natural pig casings, gouache

材料：银、天然猪肠衣、水粉

Type: Bracelet / Necklace / Shoulder piece

类型：手镯 / 项链 / 肩饰

Techniques: Thermal pigment integration, organic tension shaping

工艺：热颜料融合、有机张力塑形

By combining organic membranes with internal armatures, this work simulates the morphological transformations driven by shifting emotional landscapes. It manifests through various states of struggle, fluidity, and sharpness. The natural shrinkage of the membrane during the drying process represents a dual movement: a quest for structural support and a form of self-imposed constraint. These pieces capture the evolving forms that emerge from the emotional flux between establishing defence mechanisms and seeking the boundaries of comfort.

The translucent membrane serves as a symbol for the soul or the subconscious, while the rigid metal framework acts as a metaphor for the shackles of the material world. The progression from rounded latency to sharp confrontation is more than a physical material process — it is a visual mapping of internal reactions as they manifest into external forms.

通过将有机薄膜与内部骨架相结合，这件作品模拟了由不断变化的情感景观驱动的形态转变。它通过挣扎、流动和尖锐的各种状态来呈现。薄膜在干燥过程中的自然收缩代表着一种双重运动：对结构支撑的追寻，以及一种自我施加的约束。这些作品捕捉了在建立防御机制与寻找舒适边界之间的情感波动中涌现的演变形态。

半透明的薄膜象征着灵魂或潜意识，而坚硬的金属框架则隐喻着物质世界的枷锁。从圆润的潜伏到尖锐的对抗，不仅仅是一个物理材料过程——它是内在反应显化为外在形态的视觉映射。

About 关于

Ke Li is a contemporary jewellery artist currently based in London. She regards jewellery as a fluid narrative medium, continuously challenging the traditional definitions of materials and exploring innovative ways of wearing pieces on the body.

Her work investigates the symbiotic relationship between jewellery and the human form, specifically focusing on the emotional resonance that emerges between the wearer and the object under varying postures and states. Through the choreography of wearing and the deliberate use of sharp or rounded forms, she manifests and communicates the most subtle and delicate of human emotions.

李可是一位目前居住在伦敦的当代珠宝艺术家。她将珠宝视为一种流动的叙事媒介，不断挑战材料的传统定义，探索将作品佩戴于身体上的创新方式。

她的作品探索珠宝与人体之间的共生关系，特别关注佩戴者与物品在不同姿态和状态下产生的情感共鸣。通过佩戴的编排以及对尖锐或圆润形态的刻意运用，她呈现并传达了人类最微妙、最细腻的情感。
