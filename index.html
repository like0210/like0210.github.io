Our Story

The art of fine jewelry making

Founded in London with a passion for creating timeless beauty, Orris London represents the pinnacle of fine jewelry creation. Our journey began with a simple belief: that every piece of jewelry should tell a unique story and be crafted with the utmost attention to detail.

Today, we continue to create exquisite pieces that celebrate life's most precious moments, combining rare gemstones with innovative design to create heirlooms for the modern era.
